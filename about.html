About Dr. Kimberly Robinson

Kimberly Robinson, DNP, MSN, RN, CCM is a dedicated healthcare professional with over 25 years of clinical and leadership experience across diverse healthcare organizations and settings. She is a mother of two adult married children, a proud grandmother to three, and a devoted dog mom.

Dr. Robinson has developed a distinguished career in patient education, case management, and as a clinical nurse specialist. Her areas of expertise include plastic and aesthetic surgery, chronic wound care, skin and ostomy care, complex drug infusions, chronic disease management, and home healthcare delivery.

She has also served in managed care, utilization review, and case management across commercial and government plans, and is currently expanding her knowledge in herbal medicine and integrative health coaching.

Kimberly is a strong advocate for health equity, lifelong learning, and whole-person healing. She is especially committed to improving healthcare access and education for medically underserved, vulnerable, and marginalized populations through community and public health efforts.

Mission Statement

Angels Wellness exists to empower individuals, families, and communities through personalized health education, advocacy, and holistic support. Our mission is to inspire positive transformation, reduce chronic disease risk, and equip people with tools to thrive in mind, body, and spirit.

Angels Wellness Philosophy

At Angels Wellness, we believe in the power of connection — between the mind, body, and spirit — as essential to true healing and lifelong health. Our philosophy integrates evidence-based medicine with a heart-centered, patient-focused approach that acknowledges every person’s dignity, purpose, and potential.

Founded on Christian values, this platform includes scripture and faith-based encouragement, but is a judgment-free space. We welcome all individuals regardless of race, religion, ethnicity, gender, orientation, or political affiliation. Our goal is to support and uplift — never to exclude or impose.
