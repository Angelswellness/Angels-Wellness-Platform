About Dr. Kimberly Robinson

Dr. Kimberly Robinson, DNP, MSN, FNP, BSN, is a board-certified nurse practitioner with over 25 years of healthcare experience. As the founder of Angels Wellness, she brings a powerful blend of clinical excellence and compassionate coaching to every client relationship.

Dr. Robinson holds a Doctorate in Nursing Practice from Bradley University (2024), a Master of Science in Nursing (Family Nurse Practitioner), and a Bachelor of Science in Nursing from the University of Louisville. She is certified in Wound, Ostomy, and Continence Nursing through Emory University, with advanced expertise in skin and wound care, diabetic foot care, home infusions, and chronic disease management.

Through Angels Wellness, she empowers individuals to take control of their health using holistic lifestyle coaching, evidence-based practices, and whole-person wellness strategies that nurture the mind, body, and spirit.
