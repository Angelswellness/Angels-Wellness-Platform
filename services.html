Services Offered

Angels Wellness offers comprehensive healthcare and wellness coaching services that emphasize preventive care, lifestyle modification, and chronic disease management. All services are grounded in education—not prescription—allowing clients to make empowered, informed decisions alongside their healthcare teams.

One-on-One Coaching

  • Personalized wellness plans tailored to individual needs
  • Support for nutrition, exercise, and stress reduction
  • Education to manage chronic conditions like diabetes and obesity

Group Workshops & Online Courses

  • Chronic wound care and diabetic foot care essentials
  • Lifestyle change programs to improve weight, glucose, and mobility

Educational Materials

  • Customized resources addressing common health challenges
  • Materials that support clients’ journeys toward sustainable health changes

Specialized Support

  • Expertise in complex wound dressing and ostomy care
  • Care strategies for diabetes-related complications
  • Delivery via in-person sessions or telehealth platforms

Although Dr. Kimberly Robinson is a Nurse Practitioner, she does not prescribe medication and focuses solely on health education and non-pharmacologic interventions. She is currently pursuing a certificate in Integrative Health Coaching and aims to work collaboratively with clients’ primary care teams to reverse or improve chronic disease conditions through education and support.
