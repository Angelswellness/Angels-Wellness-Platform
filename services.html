Wholehearted Care, Mindful Focus

Angels Wellness offers comprehensive healthcare and wellness coaching services with a focus on preventive care, lifestyle modification, and chronic disease management. All services are grounded in education—not prescriptions—empowering clients to make informed decisions and achieve their best health outcomes.

One-on-One Coaching

  • Personalized nutrition plans and meal plans with macronutrients calculated for weight management and optimal health
  • Customized exercise plans: Pilates, Zumba, spin classes, yoga, chair yoga
  • Stress reduction and behavioral health change coaching, including printable plans and links to community resources for breathwork, tai chi, yoga breathing, and meditation
  • Education to manage chronic conditions such as hypertension, diabetes, obesity, and high cholesterol

Group Workshops and Telehealth Support

  • Chronic wound care and diabetic foot care essentials
  • Lifestyle change programs to improve weight, stabilize glucose, and enhance mobility
  • Behavioral health change support with a focus on mindfulness-based interventions

Educational Materials

  • Subscribe for weekly updates, educational content, and infographics addressing common health challenges
  • Materials designed to guide clients toward sustainable health changes

Specialized Support

  • Herbal remedies and Gardening 101
  • Functional medicine strategies
  • Care strategies for diabetes-related complications
  • Complex and chronic wound care

How We Work
