Discover Angels Wellness – Mind, Body & Spirit Care

At Angels Wellness, we believe true health starts with knowing your “why.” Do you want to lose weight, feel better, and look better? Do you have a family history of cardiovascular disease or other chronic conditions? Are you living with hypertension, prediabetes, pain, fatigue, or other chronic health concerns? Do you already have the information but need motivation, guidance, and accountability?

If you answered yes, Angels Wellness is here to partner with you. Through personalized health coaching, we combine evidence-based medicine with holistic, whole-person strategies that focus on nutrition, movement, mental well-being, and lifestyle practices designed to help you thrive.

Our Mind-Body-Spirit Approach

Nutrition & Whole-Food Living

Science-backed diets such as DASH, Mediterranean, and plant-based eating reduce inflammation, improve heart health, and support weight balance. Get a personalized meal plan with nutritional guidance tailored to your goals.

Exercise & Movement

Movement is medicine. Just 30 minutes of activity like walking, swimming, dancing, yoga, or gardening can improve energy and reduce stress. Receive a custom plan to improve mobility, health, and well-being.

Mental Wellness & Emotional Balance

  • Meditation and mindfulness
  • Yoga for balance and flexibility
  • Gardening and grounding in nature
  • Creative activities to reduce stress and anxiety

Sunlight, Vitamin D & Connection

Fresh air and sunshine boost vitamin D, improve mood, and strengthen immunity. Join group wellness events or 1:1 coaching for connection and vitality.

Stress Management

Learn effective tools—deep breathing, journaling, faith-based reflection, and meaningful connection—to reduce stress and improve chronic conditions.

Sleep & Restoration

Adequate, quality sleep restores body and mind. We guide you to healthy sleep routines that improve heart health and emotional well-being.

Wellness is not a destination—it’s a daily practice. With Dr. Kimberly Robinson’s heart-centered guidance, Angels Wellness helps you live with vitality, peace, and purpose.
