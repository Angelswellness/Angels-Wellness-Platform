Mind, Body, Spirit Wellness

“Do you not know that your body is a temple of the Holy Spirit, who is in you...” – 1 Corinthians 6:19

At Angels Wellness, our mission is to improve the well-being of individuals, families, and communities through education, support, and personalized care. Our philosophy is rooted in the belief that optimal health is achieved through a connectedness of the mind, body, and spirit.

Nutrition & Whole-Food Living

We promote science-backed dietary patterns such as the DASH diet, the Mediterranean diet, and plant-based eating—all known to reduce inflammation, improve cardiovascular health, and support weight balance.

Exercise & Movement

Doctors recommend at least 30 minutes of moderate exercise per day. Walking, swimming, dancing, yoga, or gardening can improve energy, reduce stress, and support cardiovascular health.

Mental Wellness

Mental health is essential to overall health. We encourage practices like:

  • Meditation and mindfulness
  • Yoga for balance and flexibility
  • Gardening and grounding to reconnect with nature
  • Creative activities to reduce anxiety, depression, and anger

Sunlight & Vitamin D

Fresh air and sunshine are powerful. Sunlight helps the body produce vitamin D, which supports bone health and mood regulation. Low vitamin D levels are associated with depression, fatigue, and increased risk of illness.

Stress Management

Stress affects every organ in the body. Managing stress through breathing, journaling, faith-based reflection, and meaningful connection is crucial for both prevention and chronic disease management—especially for heart disease and diabetes.

Sleep & Restoration

Adequate sleep is not a luxury—it's a necessity. Quality sleep reduces the risk of heart attacks, strokes, and boosts overall healing and mental clarity.

Wellness is not a destination—it’s a daily practice. Through Angels Wellness, Dr. Kimberly Robinson supports individuals with evidence-based tools and heart-centered guidance to live with vitality, peace, and purpose.
